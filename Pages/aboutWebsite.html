About This Website

Diabetes@Home (or D@H) is a website that helps people manage their diabetes and is the flagship product of the digital health startup, Diabetes@Home. Patients enter their health data on a daily basis as well as view their history in one easy-to-use portal. This information is then sent to their clinician.

Patient Features

When patients are logged in they have access to the following features:

  • Record your health data
  • Monitor how your data changes over time
  • Receive messages from your clinician
  • View the patient leaderboard

Clinician Features

When clinicians are logged in they have access to the following features:

  • View individual patient's health data
  • Monitor how patients' data changes over time
  • Send messages to your patients
  • Write patient notes
  • View all patients in a concise view
  • Informs you of any patients outside of the safety thresholds
  • Add new patients to the system
