About this website

This site is the flagship web app of Diabetes@Home, a startup commited to providing digital health solutions to the wider community. Our app services about 5,000 patients and 100 clinicians who are located in Victoria, Australia.

Okay, but what does this site actually do?

Our web app allows patients to easily record their health data. In turn, patients can track their progress and become more motivated to monitor their health.

In addition, clinicians can also track their patients through the use of this app, send personalised messages and be up-to-date with everything they need to know about a patient's condition.
